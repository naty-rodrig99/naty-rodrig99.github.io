About me

Hi, I'm Natalia

Passionate about problem-solving, mostly in the areas of Human-Computer and Human-Robot Interaction, I focus on all stages of a product: from research and ideation to design and implementation. With a strong background in UX research, software development, and AI-driven solutions, I like to create intuitive, human-centered experiences that bridge the gap between technology and users.

I bring a structured and creative approach to design, combining user insights, stakeholder collaboration, and the latest design thinking trends to build digital experiences. I have experience leading cross-functional teams, working in agile environments, and translating complex requirements into beautiful, functional interfaces. Driven by curiosity, I enjoy exploring and learning emerging technologies, ensuring that every product I work on is not just visually compelling but also strategically impactful.
